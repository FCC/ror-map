This map displays the rate-of-return study area boundaries and related geographic information the Wireline Competition Bureau plans to incorporate into the next version of the Alternative Connect America Cost Model (A-CAM). The map is based on the study area and boundary exchange data that were submitted to the Bureau and certified by rate-of-return carriers and certain state utility commissions [[link to SAB page?]]ZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZ.

The process used to develop service areas for A-CAM modeling purposes is explained in an accompanying Public Notice [[link]]ZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZZ. As described in the Public Notice, each service area has an aggregation point (central office or CO) associated with it. Service areas whose CO location is determined from proprietary GeoResults data are shown in cross-hatch; information on how to access those locations is described in the Public Notice. Service areas with another source for the CO location are shown as white dots on the map.

Interested parties can download the study area and service area polygons and the non-proprietary central office locations by selecting the area(s) of interest, hitting download, and selecting a format.
